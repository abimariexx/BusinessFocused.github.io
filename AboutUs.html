About Us

We are a group of five passionate young entrepreneurs who have come together with a mission: to support and empower entrepreneurs in Panama, helping them gain the recognition they deserve. In a competitive market, we understand the challenges local business owners face, and that's why we've created the "Business Focused" app.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet powerful: to provide entrepreneurs in Panama with the platform and tools needed to stand out and succeed in their business endeavors. We believe in the potential of local entrepreneurs and are committed to being their ally on the path to success.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a thriving entrepreneurial ecosystem in Panama, where every business, regardless of its size, has the opportunity to flourish and make a significant impact. We aspire to be the go-to platform for entrepreneurs, offering not only the tools for success but also a supportive community that fosters growth, collaboration, and innovation.

What is "Business Focused"?

"Business Focused" is more than just an app; it's a community. Our platform is specifically designed for entrepreneurs and small businesses. We offer a space where entrepreneurs can showcase their products and services prominently, connect with a broader audience, and boost their sales. Moreover, we provide a range of tools and resources, including marketing support, analytics, and expert advice to help businesses thrive and grow.

Our Commitment

We are committed to delivering high-quality service to our users. We value innovation, integrity, and excellence in everything we do. Our goal is to be a trusted partner for entrepreneurs and contribute to the economic development of Panama.
